## Content Sourcing Rules

1. **Same-Day Only** - Only use articles published within last 24 hours
2. **Multiple Sources** - Combine insights from 2-3 sources per article
3. **Original Rewrite** - Never copy/paste. Rewrite with Growth Pulse voice
4. **Trending Topics** - Prioritize topics appearing across multiple sources
5. **Unique Angle** - Add original analysis, not just regurgitation

## Article Creation Workflow

### Step 1: Source Monitoring (Daily)
- Check RSS feeds every morning
- Identify trending topics across sources
- Select 3-5 most relevant stories

### Step 2: Research (Same Day)
- Read original articles
- Note key insights and data
- Identify gaps or counterpoints

### Step 3: Original Writing
- Rewrite with Growth Pulse voice (witty, casual, educational)
- Add original analysis and examples
- Create unique H2 headers
- No em dashes

### Step 4: Image Generation (GPT-1.5)
- Create photorealistic iPhone 16 style image
- Must be relevant to article content

### Step 5: Publish
- Add to posts.json
- Commit and push
- Vercel auto-deploys

## Tone Guidelines

- **Voice:** Witty, sharp, slightly irreverent
- **Style:** Casual but informed, like talking to a smart friend
- **Humor:** Dry humor, occasional sarcasm, never mean-spirited
- **Education:** Every article must teach something valuable
- **Length:** 400-600 words
- **H2 Headers:** Must be unique per article (no templates)
